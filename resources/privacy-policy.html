Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Moz Battlegrounds is a private multiplayer game for friends. We prioritize your privacy and collect minimal data necessary to provide the service.

Information We Collect

When you use Moz Battlegrounds, we may collect:

  • Username: The display name you choose when playing
  • Game Data: In-game actions and statistics during gameplay
  • Technical Data: Basic connection information (IP address) for game functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Enable multiplayer functionality
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs

Data Storage

Game data is stored temporarily during active game sessions. We do not maintain long-term databases of personal information. Your username and preferences are stored locally in your browser.

Third-Party Services

Moz Battlegrounds is hosted on Render.com. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Choose any username (no account required)
  • Clear your local browser data at any time
  • Stop using the service at any time

Children's Privacy

Moz Battlegrounds is intended for use among friends. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through the game's community channels.

Moz Battlegrounds is based on OpenFront.IO, an open source multiplayer strategy game.
